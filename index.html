Objectives
  • To form, establish and provide a single united organization of all individuals and agro-related organizations in the district with full knowledge, authority and capacity to represent, deliberate and make binding resolutions regarding the sector.
  • To develop, maintain and promote farmers network in the district to ensure effective participation of farmers, peasants, agriculturalists and all people in the agro-related business at all levels in the district.
  • To harness the abundant water resources in the district in developing irrigation so that our agricultural system ceases depending on natural rains only.
  • To improve the health standards of the farming community through control of HIV/ AIDS, malaria and other communicable diseases.
